Money Trees

Beautiful trees, grow tall and/or bushy depending on care.

  • Water when soil is 75% dry
  • Bright and indirect light
  • Too much direct light will burn the leaves

Bolivian Turtle Vine

Beautiful metallic looking leaves, bright red flowers, grows as a falling vine.

  • Water often, keep soil moist
  • Bright indirect light
  • Bottom water to avoid letting water touch the leaves

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Very pretty plant, grows tall and strong, leaves are quite thick.

  • Needs consistent watering
  • Requires bright light
